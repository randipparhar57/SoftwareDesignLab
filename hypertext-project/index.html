B-Sides

December 2021 to February 2022

Vision: On the top, there is navbar. There will be four tabs, each one representing a different period of time in my life over the past year. There were a lot of images, so I narrowed down to ones that best capture the way I saw the world and the feelings of that period. If you hover over the images, text will appear. The text will either be lyrics of songs that I listened to during that time, journal entries, and quotes from books that resonate with the mood of that time period. I would like to incorporate some way of a playlist automatically playing when you are on different tabs. Like, there will be a pause/play/volume control icon on the corner somewhere and a playlist will just play in the background - music that I listened to a lot during that period which coincides with the mood that was being experienced. Aestheically, it is a work in progress. I will look for a custom font that I like, change the background to a blurred image from that time period to add to the ambience for lack of better word. I want there to be a fading out as you scroll up and fading in as you scroll down the page. But, very rough draft of what I'm trying to accomplish. Very far from the final vision I have, but yeah that's about it.
